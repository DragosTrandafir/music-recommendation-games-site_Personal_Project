Why is this my favourite album?

Honeymoon has a special vibe for me. From the first song to the last one, you feel a fierceful dreamy, cinematic message which lays both in the lyrics and the background of every song. Furthermore, for me Honeymoon actually feels like a cloudy day in a hot summer day of July at the beach. So, it always makes me think of the summer vacation. Thus, the album also brings along calmness and melancholia, because the long summer vacations are associated, by me at least, with the childhood and adolescence in general

Consequently, should my own experience be taken into consideration, I highly recommend you to listen to the entire album at least twice.

I am sure that at least "Music To Watch Boys To", "Art Deco" and "High By The Beach" will grab your attention, and will make you come back to this album quite often.
