Lana del Rey - The artist who influenced my adolescence

Lana Del Rey is the stage name of American singer, songwriter and songwriter Elizabeth Woolridge Grant. He was born on June 21, 1985 in New York City, USA. Del Rey has gained tremendous recognition and fame for her unique style of music that combines elements of pop, rock and indie music with a unique vintage and nostalgic aesthetic.

Lana Del Rey is known for her warm and melancholic music, as well as her frequently cinematic and sensual music videos. His songs often explore themes of love, fame, Americana and nostalgia. He has a devoted fanbase and has been influential in the new and indie music scene.

My personal discovery regarding her music

When I was about 15 years old, a good friend of mine told me I should definitely listen to this artist. Since then, I continuously digged deeper and deeper into Lana's discography, being astounded by her cinematic tracks, but also relentessly soaking up her melancholia and diversity transmitted through her art. Her music made me realize the importance of loyality and commitment in relationship, and that love is a fierceful feeling...

I consider of the utmost importance that a bit patience is needed for some songs, because every album has songs which are more or less catchy from the first listen. However, I believe that every album brings you in another universe...
